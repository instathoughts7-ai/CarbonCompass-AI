# 🌱 CarbonCompass AI

## Your AI Guide to Understanding, Tracking, and Reducing Your Carbon Footprint

CarbonCompass AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, interactive simulations, and intelligent recommendations.

The platform transforms complex environmental data into actionable guidance, empowering users to make informed decisions that positively impact the planet.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Many people want to live more sustainably but struggle to understand how their daily choices affect the environment.

CarbonCompass AI solves this challenge by:

* Measuring personal carbon footprints
* Identifying hidden emission drivers
* Simulating lifestyle changes
* Providing AI-powered sustainability recommendations
* Tracking long-term environmental progress
