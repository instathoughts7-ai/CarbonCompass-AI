# 🌱 CarbonCompass AI

### Your AI Guide to Understanding, Tracking, and Reducing Your Carbon Footprint

CarbonCompass AI is an intelligent sustainability companion that helps individuals understand their environmental impact, track carbon emissions over time, and discover personalized actions to reduce their footprint. Using AI-powered insights, carbon simulations, and progress tracking, the platform transforms complex sustainability data into simple, actionable recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

CarbonCompass AI addresses this challenge by providing:

* Personalized carbon footprint assessments
* AI-generated sustainability insights
* Carbon reduction recommendations
* Lifestyle impact simulations
* Progress tracking and goal setting
* Long-term carbon profile management

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📊 Carbon Footprint Assessment

Analyze emissions across:

* Transportation
* Flights
* Household Energy
* Diet & Food Choices
* Shopping Habits
* Waste Management

### 🧭 Personal Carbon Compass

Generate a personalized sustainability profile including:

* Carbon Score
* Risk Classification
* Emissions Breakdown
* Impact Analysis

### 🤖 AI Sustainability Insights

Powered by Google Gemini AI:

* Hidden emission driver detection
* Personalized reduction strategies
* Behavioral recommendations
* Environmental impact forecasting

### 🔄 What-If Simulator

Explore lifestyle changes before making them:

* Reduce driving
* Change diet preferences
* Lower electricity usage
* Reduce flights
* Compare carbon savings

### 📈 Progress Tracking

Monitor long-term improvement through:

* Historical snapshots
* Emission trends
* Goal tracking
* Sustainability streaks

### 💾 Carbon Profile Backup

Import and export complete Carbon Profiles using secure JSON backups.
