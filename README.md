# 🌱 CarbonCompass AI

## Your AI Guide to Understanding, Tracking, and Reducing Your Carbon Footprint

CarbonCompass AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, intelligent recommendations, interactive simulations, and long-term progress tracking.

The platform transforms complex environmental data into simple, actionable guidance that empowers users to make more sustainable lifestyle decisions.

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Individuals often struggle to understand how daily activities contribute to their carbon footprint and which actions will have the greatest environmental impact.

CarbonCompass AI solves this problem by providing:

* Personalized carbon footprint assessments
* AI-powered sustainability insights
* Carbon reduction recommendations
* Lifestyle impact simulations
* Progress tracking and historical analysis
* Secure cloud synchronization and backups
